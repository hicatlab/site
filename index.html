Research Projects

Our work spans tangible user interfaces, visualizations, creative systems, and human-centered AI.

AI Creativity
SAGE3 - Collaboration and Brainstorming

SAGE3 is a collaboration software that boasts embracing spatial workflows. Now, with LLM and AI capabilities, we study how creative activities, like brainstorming, can benefit from the spatialized paradigm.

View Project →
3D Printing Visualization
DEM Cropper

We often 3D print large models of terrain for visualization exhibits. This tool is meant to help with creating the models for such prints from Digital Elevation Model (DEM) files.

View Project →

ClimaTales

With so much cliamte data available, how do we reach people with important observations? ClimaTales combines projected data layers with currated data narratives.
